How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → AresFit → Cancel.

Is the barcode scanner free?

Yes, the barcode scanner and food search are completely free for all users.

How accurate is the calorie tracking?

AresFit uses USDA FoodData Central and Open Food Facts — the same databases used by leading nutrition apps — plus a 7-day calibration period to learn your actual metabolism.
