# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 23, 2026

AresFit ("we", "us", "our") operates the AresFit mobile application (the "App"). This page tells you what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Information We Collect

**Information you give us:**
- Account info (name, email, username, profile photo)
- Health info (weight, height, age, sex, fitness goals, workouts, meals, water intake, sleep)
- Photos you upload (progress photos, profile pictures)

**Information collected automatically:**
- Device info (model, iOS version, language)
- App usage (features used, crash logs)

**Apple Health data:**
- If you allow it, we read steps, active calories, weight, sleep, and workout data from Apple Health, and write your logged workouts, weight, and water back to Apple Health.
- This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers unless you sync it to your squad.

## How We Use Your Information

- To run the App and its features (meal tracking, workouts, squad features, etc.)
- To personalize your experience (AI meal plans, workout suggestions)
- To improve the App (crash reports, analytics)
- To send notifications you've opted into

## How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell your data.

We share data only with:
- **Supabase** — our database provider (stores your account + squad info)
- **Anthropic** — powers the AI coach and meal planner (only when you use those features)
- **Apple** — for in-app purchases and Apple Health
- **USDA FoodData Central** — for food database lookups (no personal info shared)

## Your Rights

- View, edit, or delete your data anytime in the Profile tab
- Delete your account by emailing us (address below)
- Turn off any data sharing (notifications, Apple Health) in iOS Settings

## Children

AresFit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) to protect your data in transit. No method is 100% secure, so we can't guarantee absolute security.

## Changes

We may update this policy. We'll notify you in the App if we do.
